Major Qualifying Project (MQP): Soft Robotic Eel

  • Team Name: Electronic Eel Locomotion Soft Water Anguilliform Robotic Device (EELSWARD)
  • Designed and developed a tendon-actuated, cable-driven soft robotic eel featuring modular, 3D-printed accordion segments made of thermoplastic elastomer (NinjaTek Chinchilla).
  • Implemented a pulley-cable actuation system powered by servo motors to replicate anguilliform swimming motion for flexible underwater locomotion.
  • Enhanced mechanical performance and modularity for easy repair, extension, and future sensor/depth control integration.
  • Conducted tank and pool testing to evaluate swim speed and durability in chlorinated water.
  • Project contributes to biological understanding of eel locomotion and potential applications in underwater exploration.
