I’m a robotics engineer with a passion for bioinspired design, intelligent systems, and hands-on innovation. I recently graduated from WPI with a B.S. in Robotics Engineering, where I worked on projects ranging from soft underwater robots to autonomous navigation and AI policy research.

I've competed internationally and contributed to both technical and creative initiatives—from building advanced robotic systems to editing a literary magazine and speaking publicly on AI. Major Qualifying Project (MQP): Soft Robotic Eel
